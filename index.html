It longed to be dipped in a vat of hot oil and fried to perfection, or maybe even smothered in glaze and sprinkles. But alas, its dreams were short-lived as it was soon gobbled up by a hungry customer who couldn't resist its charms.
And so, the donut's journey came to an end, but at least it got to live its life to the fullest before being devoured.
